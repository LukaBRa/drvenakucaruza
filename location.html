ROSE'S LOCATION

Weekend resort

 Weekend resort Kopaonik is located 3-7km from the center, altitude of 1200-1500m, along the main road Raska-Kopaonik which is in snow conditions constantly cleaned and maintained. The resort has large accommodation capacity that provides guests a pleasant stay and a beautiful view of the mountains slopes. In pleasant peace this is the perfect location for those who want to escape from the noisy everyday life and enjoy nature.
 Wooden house Rose is located 4km from the center, 1.7km from the first ropeway (Sunny Valley) at an altitude of 1400m and the place where the wind rose blows in the quietest part of the Weekend resort.
