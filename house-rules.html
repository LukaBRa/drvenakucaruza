House rules of the SPA center

 We expect you to treat the contents of the spa center as responsible guests and consciously abide to the house rules.

 PEACE AND SILENCE ARE THE BASIC CONDITIONS OF STAYING IN THE SPA CENTER WHICH IS THERE FOR YOUR REST AND RELAXATION.

  • All guests accommodated in the “WOODEN HOUSE ROSE” with the rental of apartments have free use of the spa center (sauna, steam bath, jacuzzi and salt room) from 17 to 22h.
  • For an additional fee, guests can be provided with a stay in the spa center outside the above-mentionded period.Info 064 15 333 72
  • Smoking is prohibited in the SPA zone.
  • It is forbidden to bring food, drinks, things with a strong and unpleasant smell, glass and metal objects.
  • Use of all SPA facilities is at your own risk.
  • Showering is mandatory before and especially after leaving the sauna and steam bath.
  • In saunas, it is mandatory to use a towel on which you will sit or lie down.
  • Pay attention to the privacy of other guests. It is strictly forbidden to make noise and inappropriate behavior.

DISCLAIMER:

  • Disappearance of personal belongings.
  • Injuries within the complex/SPA that occurred due to carelessness of the guests or non-compliance with house rules
  • Safety of vehicles parked within the complex.
